Grace Davidson

Founder, Grace Davidson Studio

Grace Davidson Studio was founded by Grace Davidson, a London based interior designer with a first class honours degree in Product Design and further training in interior design and software, including AutoCAD 2D, SketchUp and 3D rendering. Her lifelong immersion in the antiques trade through her family’s specialist antique lighting business gives her an instinctive eye for the pieces that make a space feel truly considered.

Grace has a deep understanding of how people interact with the things around them, and that thinking runs through every project she takes on. How a room flows. How light moves through a space. How a piece of joinery sits within a room, not just as a functional object but as something considered, crafted, and integral to the whole. Every material, every finish, every detail exists in relation to everything else.

She believes that incorporating antique and vintage pieces alongside contemporary design is what transforms a space from beautiful to truly memorable. Access to exceptional vintage pieces that you simply would not find on the high street is something she brings to every project.

Great interiors are built on great relationships. Grace takes the time to understand how you live, what you love, and the feeling you want your home to have. From initial layouts through to the finest details, layering texture, natural materials, and considered touches of colour alongside bespoke joinery and carefully sourced pieces. The result is a home that feels full of life and personality, functional and beautiful, and entirely your own.
