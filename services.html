Our most immersive service, built for clients who want their home to feel extraordinary without the complexity of managing it themselves. From the first conversation to the final styling touch, we handle everything, so the only thing you need to do is enjoy the result.

This service is built on a close working relationship. We take the time to understand not just how your home looks, but how it feels to live in, and we design around that.

We start by getting to know you, your home, and how you want it to feel. This is the foundation everything else is built on.
We develop the creative direction for your space: mood boards, colour palettes, materials, layout, and the overall feel of each room.
Furniture, fabrics, lighting, and finishes are refined in detail and presented for your sign-off.
We manage all ordering, supplier communication, lead times, and deliveries on your behalf.
Everything comes together on site. Your home is installed, styled, and ready for you to move into.
